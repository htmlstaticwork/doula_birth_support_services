Common Questions

A doula provides continuous physical comfort (massage, positioning, counter-pressure), emotional steadying, and informational support. We help bridge the gap between medical staff and your personal birth goals.

Most families hire their doula between 20-30 weeks of pregnancy. This allows time for prenatal visits and relationship building. However, we have also supported families who reached out in their final weeks!
