Pricing & Financial FAQ

Answering common questions about the financial side of doula care.

Yes, in most cases doula services are considered a qualified medical expense. We can provide you with an itemized receipt (superbill) for reimbursement from your health savings or flexible spending account.

We typically require a 30% retainer to secure your due date, with the remaining balance due by your 37th week of pregnancy. Custom payment schedules can be arranged if needed.

Equity is central to our mission. We offer a limited number of sliding-scale slots each month and partner with local community organizations to provide subsidized care for those who qualify.
