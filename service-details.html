The Journey with Your Doula

Prenatal Meetings

Two detailed sessions in your home to create a birth plan, practice comfort measures, and discuss any concerns or past birth trauma.

On-Call Period

Starting at 37 weeks, your doula is on-call 24/7. When labor begins, we are available by phone and will join you at home or the hospital as soon as you are ready for physical support.

Continuous Labor Support

We stay by your side until after the baby is born. We provide massage, hip squeezes, position suggestions, and reminders of your wishes.

Early Newborn Support

We assist with the initial breastfeeding/bonding period and stay until you are settled comfortably in your postpartum room.
